Cheese on Cheese

Lasagna:A baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Place even layers of the three ingredients onto a pan until it is full
  2. Heat in oven at 450 for 30 minutes
  3. Take out of oven and let cool before serving
